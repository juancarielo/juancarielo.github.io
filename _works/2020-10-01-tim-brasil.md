Also at **everis**, I supported **TIM Brasil's** institutional site, one of Brazil's largest telecom operators, built on a _hybrid_ Drupal 9 architecture using ~~Cohesion~~ Site Studio, on Acquia Cloud infrastructure.

My contribution centered on custom development consuming external **Web Components** and assembling them into the front-end with ~~Cohesion~~ Site Studio, alongside **SSO** integration for authentication, supporting this large-scale, high-traffic telecom platform.

This included modeling **content types and fields** in Drupal to structure coverage and plans data, exposed through **APIs** and consumed by the Web Components to render dynamic pages across the site.

Content was also organized by **region**, allowing different areas of Brazil to have their own tailored pages and information. 

This structure empowered the **marketing and business teams** to edit and publish regional content themselves through Site Studio, without depending on developers for routine updates.
