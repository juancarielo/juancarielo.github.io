Also through **Jussi**, I worked on **Honda's** automobiles portal, another site within the same _coupled_, multisite Drupal 8 architecture built with Symfony components.

The site acted as Honda's digital showroom, presenting the full car line-up with their respective versions, trims, pricing, technical data sheets, image galleries, and promotional videos, along with tools to help buyers compare models and locate dealerships.

On the back end, I developed custom modules and API integrations to structure this vehicle catalog, handling frequent content updates whenever new models, versions, or price tables were released across the line-up.
