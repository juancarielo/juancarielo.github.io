Working with **TCS** as a **Drupal Developer**, I contributed to the **Johnson & Johnson Institute** platform, a _traditional_, _multilingual_ Drupal 9 site on Acquia Cloud, built to deliver health programs, training content, and manuals to medical professionals worldwide.

Day-to-day, I built numerous **custom modules** to support platform-specific features, configured multilingual content workflows and language negotiation across the site's several locales. 

Code quality was enforced with **SonarQube**, search and content indexing ran on **Apache Solr**, dependencies were managed with **Composer**, and all changes went through **pull requests** and peer review before merging.

Collaborating remotely with a team in India 🇮🇳, this was also my first time using English professionally in a work setting.
