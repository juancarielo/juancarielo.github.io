At **Vorttex**, my first contact with **Drupal 7** marked the start of what would become my specialization in the Drupal ecosystem, working on it alongside other projects as a **Developer**.

The main one was the **ClosetRank API**, a **RESTful** back-end made with **Laravel** and **MySQL**, secured with **JWT authentication** to serve a mobile/web application.

I designed the database schema using Laravel migrations to keep the structure versioned and consistent across environments, then exposed the data through JSON endpoints consumed by the front-end client.

Working as the sole back-end developer, I sharpened my skills in API design, authentication flows, and structuring a Laravel application from the ground up.
