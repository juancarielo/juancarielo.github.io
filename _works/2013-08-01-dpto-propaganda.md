**DPTO Propaganda**, an advertising agency, was my first job as a developer, where I worked as a **Junior Developer** developing and maintaining internal systems and client sites using **CakePHP** (MVC) and **MySQL**, with infrastructure hosted on **AWS**.

Beyond writing code, I was involved in database modeling from the ground up and got exposed to the business side of client projects, learning how technical decisions tied into deadlines, budgets, and campaign needs.

This role built the foundation of my back-end engineering career, from structuring relational databases to shipping and maintaining production applications.
