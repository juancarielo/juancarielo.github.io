At **everis**, as a **Drupal Developer**, I worked on **Amil's** institutional site, one of Brazil's largest healthcare and insurance providers, built on a _fully decoupled_ architecture with Drupal 8 and React, running on Acquia Cloud.

My role covered exposing content through Drupal's API layer, while React on the front end consumed that data to deliver a fast, dynamic experience for this high-traffic institutional site.

Working **headless**, Drupal handled content modeling and editorial workflows while serving structured content through **JSON:API** endpoints, decoupled from any front-end rendering. On top of that, I built and maintained custom **REST resources** to shape and expose data beyond the default endpoints, tailoring payloads to match what the React application needed for each page and component.
