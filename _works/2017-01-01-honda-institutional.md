Through **Jussi**, I worked as a **Drupal Developer** on **Honda's** institutional portal, part of a _coupled_, multisite Drupal 8 architecture built with Symfony components and hosted on Acquia Cloud.

Functioning as the brand's central hub, the site covered company history, corporate values, sustainability and environmental initiatives, press releases, motorsport activities, and the dealer network across Brazil.

I built and maintained several custom modules and API integrations behind the scenes, handling everything from press release workflows to sustainability reports, so editorial teams could publish corporate content quickly and reliably.
