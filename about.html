Solutions Engineer & Drupal Specialist

With over 12 years of experience in software engineering and web development, I have established a solid career focused on systems architecture, back-end engineering, and the delivery of high-performance enterprise portals. My core specialty is built on more than 7 years of deep engagement within the Drupal ecosystem, leading the development of robust, scalable, and secure platforms for the enterprise market.

Currently, as a Solutions Engineer (Pre-Sales) at Acquia LatAm, I leverage this advanced technical background as a strategic business differentiator. My role bridges the gap between software engineering and the sales process, supporting commercial teams by translating complex business challenges into viable technical solutions. I am responsible for handling technical discoveries, designing efficient architectures, mitigating risks, delivering high-impact PoCs/demos, and demonstrating the real-world value of Acquia's DXP (Digital Experience Platform) portfolio, closely aligning platform capabilities with each client's unique needs.

Throughout my career, I have developed a strong ability to transition seamlessly between low-level code discussions with development teams and executive presentations for C-level decision-makers. My technical background includes driving enterprise-level implementations for major brands like CPFL, Johnson & Johnson, Honda, Amil (UnitedHealth Group), and TIM, utilizing advanced modern tech stacks, CI/CD pipelines, decoupled architectures, and AI integrations.

Looking back at where it all started, my roots in technology trace back to early web engineering, writing raw PHP, managing full-stack environments, and developing applications for digital advertising campaigns. This deep, hands-on foundation in the trenches of pure web development is what allows me today to quickly unmask complex modern infrastructure challenges and confidently guide enterprise clients toward successful digital transformations.

Core Competencies: Solutions Engineering (Pre-Sales), Software Architecture, Acquia DXP Ecosystem (Cloud Platform, Code Studio), Advanced PHP Engineering, Drupal (7, 8, 9, 10, 11 & CMS), Decoupled Architectures, Technical Strategy, and Consultative Selling.
